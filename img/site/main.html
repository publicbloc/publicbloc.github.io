Public Bloc

Taking Project Funding to Blockchain

The Grand Vision

We are looking to innovate how businesses receive funding for their projects on a global scale.

Public Bloc is setting out to create a user-friendly, web-based Blockchain solution that will allow investors to connect directly with businesses both domestically and internationally. We believe that this platform will enable countless organisations find the assets they require in order to enhance their functionality and increase their profitability.

Within our current timeline, we estimate a full platform to be developed and ready for deployment sometime within Q2 2018. Although a handful of other companies have begun to research this opportunity, we believe we are at the forefront of beginning to enter development of a tangible product.

The Technology

Security

In order to create a flushed-out platform for international investors to fund projects, we strive to implement key security features in our platform. This is meant to ensure that all parties will know that their assets are in safe hands.

Funding

The purpose of Public Bloc is to allow people to find funding for their projects from outside of their country. It is a platform that will revolutionalise the way investors and businesses conduct trade both domestically and internationally.

Functionality

Our developers are setting out to deliver a streamlined, user-friendly experience to all of our users. This means that users will be able to access their account from their desktops and phones, in order to view their information on the go.
